## Combat (js/combat.js)
- Combo system: chain jab→cross→hook for bonus damage
- Counter-attack mechanic: time block during enemy attack for free hit
- Grab/clinch mechanic with stamina drain
- Critical hit system (random chance for 2x damage)
- Dodge/evade with i-frames
- **Stamina Break: opponent at 0 stamina staggers, taking 1.5x damage for 2 seconds**
- **Rage Mode: bosses get second wind at 25% HP — 30% faster attacks but 20% less defense**
- **Signature Move: unlock a unique finishing move after defeating each boss enemy**
- **Taunt System: taunt enemies to lower their defense (-15% for 3s) and build Focus meter**
- **Focus/Chi Meter: builds on landed hits/perfect blocks, fuels counter-breakers and super attacks**
- **Limb Targeting: strategic body hits — leg shots slow movement, arm shots reduce grapple damage**
- **Pressure System: consecutive landed hits build Pressure meter — at 100%, next attack deals 2.5x damage and breaks guard**
- **Stun Chain: landing 5+ hits in a combo applies stun (enemies can't attack for 1s), resets on enemy hit or timeout**
- **Breaking Point: sustained damage without landing hits causes enemy defense to drop 25% and increases stun vulnerability — visually shown with cracking animation**
- **Chain Throw System: grappler enemies chain grabs together — must mash escape button or face a devastating submission combo (armbar→triangle→leg lock)**
- **Focus Fire Mode: hold both triggers to slow time and guarantee critical hit on next attack — takes 25% increased damage while active**
- **Momentum System: landing hits builds momentum (max 5 stacks) — each stack adds +5% damage and +2% crit chance; getting hit or missing resets momentum. At 5 stacks, trigger MOMENTUM SURGE: next attack is auto-crit with +50% damage.**
- **Grapple Escape QTE: when caught in clinch/submission, rapid button prompt appears — mash to escape. Escape speed scales with player speed stat. Failed escape = full damage from hold.**
- **Super Armored Counter: hold block while attacking to tank through incoming hits — if hit during charge, auto-counter triggers dealing 2x damage and building extra Focus**
- **Finisher Showcase: on KO, trigger slow-mo replay showing the final sequence from 3 angles — left, right, center — with "FINISH!" text overlay and score bonus based on style**
- **Injury System: body part tracking — cumulative limb hits apply stacking debuffs (arm hits = -10% attack speed per stack, leg hits = -5% movement speed per stack) that persist until room clear**
- **Breathing Rhythm System: rapid actions cause "winded" state — stamina regen reduced 50% for 5s. Player can perform "Breathing Technique" (hold block for 2s uninterrupted) to recover. Enemies also get winded when forced to overcommit, causing missed attacks and openings.**
- **Mental Pressure System: clean hits (unblocked), Taunts, and finishing moves build "Mental Break" meter. At 100%, enemy enters panic — 30% miss chance, -25% defense, refuses to pursue for 2s. Distinct from physical Pressure system.**
- **Fighter's Intuition: passive meter builds during extended fights (45+ sec vs same enemy). Full meter triggers "flow state" — next attack auto-counters perfectly, costs 0 stamina, deals 1.5x damage with slow-mo effect. Resets after trigger or fight end.**
- **Mirror Match Protocol: when player HP drops below 30%, enemy gains temporary copy of player's last 3 used attacks (mirrors strategy). Player can exploit by sequencing weak attacks to confuse enemy AI — strategic mind games at low HP.**
- **Clinch Escape Matrix: different escape timing windows for different grab types — tap direction matches the grab (high grab = up, low grab = down, middle = any direction). Creates rock-paper-scissors read gameplay.**
- **Adrenaline Rush: landing a perfect counter-attack triggers Adrenaline — next attack costs 0 stamina, deals 1.3x damage, and ignores 50% of enemy defense. Resets after trigger or 5 seconds.**
- **Weapon Clash: when attack animations intersect mid-frame, trigger clash animation (sparks + lockup) — player must time a follow-up press within 500ms to knock enemy back, or get knocked back themselves. High-risk high-reward timing mechanic.**
- **Finish Him Mode: when enemy HP drops below 10%, display "FINISH HIM!" prompt — next damaging attack triggers dramatic KO sequence with screen flash, slow-motion impact, and 2x score bonus.**
- **Deadly Windows: each enemy attack leaves a vulnerability window during recovery frames where counter-attacks deal 1.5x damage. Different moves have different window lengths (quick jabs = 200ms, slow haymakers = 500ms). Visual cue: enemy flashes red during window.**
- **Breakthrough System: guard breaks after blocking too many hits within 3 seconds — player enters "Guard Broken" state lasting 3s where blocking is disabled but attacks gain +25% damage and +10% movement speed as compensation. Recovery also restores +10 Focus.**
- **Exploit Recovery: after dodging an enemy attack, a 400ms "exploit window" opens — next attack within this window deals +30% damage and has guaranteed stun chance. Rewards reactive play over button mashing.**
- **Rhythm Chain System: fighting moves sync with zone background music BPM — landing attacks in rhythm builds "Rhythm Meter". At 100%, next attack auto-crits with visual beat-pulse effect. Different zones have different BPM (gym = 100, arena = 130, underground = 90). Rhythm bonus stacks with Momentum.**
- **Flash KO Blindness: dramatic KO causes camera flash that leaves enemy temporarily blinded in next room (if same enemy type) — 30% miss chance for 10 seconds, visual white afterimage effect**
- **Momentum Shift Counter: after getting hit 3+ times consecutively, a successful block triggers Momentum Shift — reverses combat flow, grants +20% damage for 5 seconds with screen edge cyan flash**
- **Staredown Intimidation: 3-second pre-fight staredown where rapid button mashing builds Intimidation meter — higher intimidation = enemy starts shaken (-5% defense, +5% player crit chance). Both player and enemy sprites show tension animations.**

## Enemies (js/enemies.js)
- New enemy types: Kickboxer (long range), Wrestler (grapple-focused), Striker (fast combos)
- Boss encounters with unique attack patterns and phases
- Enemy difficulty scaling per zone
- Pack behavior: enemies coordinate attacks
- Flee behavior when low HP
- Submission AI: grappler enemies attempt armbars/triangles — must interrupt within 2s or lose fight
- **Rival System: recurring boss "Shadow" that appears across multiple zones with scaling stats — dialogue changes based on player's fighting style**
- **Elite Variants: stronger versions of base enemies (Elite Kickboxer, Elite Wrestler) with 2x HP, unique color glow, drop rare items**
- **Coach Enemy: support-type enemy that shouts commands to boost nearby allies (+15% attack speed per Coach in room). Prioritize to disable enemy coordination.**
- **Mercenary Contracts: optional boss modifiers — pay in-game currency before fight for harder enemies but guaranteed rare loot drop. Scales: Bronze (+25% HP/drops), Silver (+50%), Gold (+100%).**
- **Tag Team AI: paired enemies "tag" mid-fight — while one attacks, other recovers stamina and repositions. Breaks solo enemy patterns. Player must separate or focus one to prevent coordinated assault.**
- **Adaptive Tactics: enemy analyzes player's last 5 attacks and gains +15% defense against repeated move types. Forces player to vary combos and prevents dominant strategy exploitation.**
- **Feint Master: special enemy type that performs fake-out attacks — initial swing misses but sets up real attack 300ms later. Must be ready to counter the follow-up or get caught.**
- **Bully AI: enemies that aggressively pursue when player HP is low, but panic-flee when their own HP drops below 20%. Creates dramatic fight dynamics.**
- **Loyalty Bond: some enemies refuse to abandon fallen allies — when a nearby enemy falls, survivors enter "vengeance mode" (+20% damage but -15% defense) with visible rage visual. Exploit by isolating enemies to prevent bond activation.**
- **Tutor Enemy: special trainer-type enemy that teaches player a technique upon victory — lose and the enemy learns player's last 3 attack patterns and uses them. Creates risk/reward to engage or avoid.**
- **Gimmick Specialist: enemy with one unusual trait (fire immunity, teleportation, regeneration) — requires adapting strategy. Distinct visual cue (elemental aura, glitch effect, health bar glow)**
- **Gang Up Coordination: when 3+ enemies are alive, they can trigger coordinated attacks — one enemy stuns player (distracts) while others flank and attack from sides. Visual warning indicator appears 1s before trigger. Rewards single-target focus or area attacks.**
- **Comeback Kid: passive triggered when losing a fight — next time facing same enemy archetype, player starts with +1 Focus meter and enemy starts with -10% HP. Rewards persistence and creates personal rivalries with specific enemy types.**

- Zone 3: Beach/outdoor training area
- Zone 4: Championship arena
- Secret rooms with bonus loot

- **Crowd Dynamics: crowd mood affects player stats — hype up the crowd for +10% damage**
- Dynamic day/night cycle in outdoor zones: affects visibility and crowd energy
- **Training Room: minigame zone where you spar with trainers to earn permanent stat boosts — speed drill, accuracy challenge, endurance test**
- **Champion's Dojo: special arena unlocked after beating Zone 4 — endless waves, high-score leaderboard, rare equipment drops**
- **Environmental Finishers: in arena zones, slam enemies into ropes/posts for 1.5x bonus damage — triggers unique KO animation**
- **Weight Class System: different zones have weight class restrictions — light/medium/heavy affects speed vs power tradeoffs**
- **Corner Pressure: in arena zones, when player is cornered (back against ropes), they take +10% damage but deal +15% damage when they push through. Visual cue: rope shake effect.**
- **Ring Out KO: in championship arena, slam enemy hard enough to force them over ropes = instant KO with unique "RING OUT" announcement.**
- **Endless Champion's Tower: 50-floor roguelike climb in Champion's Dojo — each floor is a random elite enemy encounter, HP restores between floors, floor 25 and 50 have boss rewards**
- **Crowd Bribe Items: purchasable items that pay specific crowd sections to cheer (+15% damage) or jeer enemy (-10% enemy accuracy) for the next room**
- **Technique Fusion Lab: special zone after Zone 3 — fuse two learned signature techniques into a hybrid move (e.g., spinning backfist + leg sweep = spinning sweep kick). Fusions inherit 70% power of each parent, add unique properties.**
- **Title Defense Mode: after beating Zone 4 championship, defend your belt against escalating challenger bosses — each defense increases title rank (Bronze→Silver→Gold→Legend). Losing = title lost, can reclaim in rematch.**
- **Corner Man System: between rooms in arena zones, consult your corner for tactical advice — choose from 3 options: "Stay aggressive" (+10% damage 3 rooms), "Play safe" (+10% defense 3 rooms), "Stamina focus" (+20% stamina regen 3 rooms). Resets each zone.**
- **Referee Judging System: in championship fights, referees score rounds based on damage dealt/landed strikes/grappling control time. Win by KO, submission, or decision (most rounds). Adds strategic layer — can't just survive, must dominate.**
- **Ring Side Power-Ups: in arena zones, temporary buff items spawn at ring corners during combat — grab first aid spray (+20 HP), energy drink (+30 stamina), focus stimulant (+full Focus meter). One item per corner per fight.**
- **Survival Time Attack: special room variant where player must survive against escalating enemy waves for 90 seconds — damage dealt = score, time survived = bonus currency. High-risk high-reward.**
- **Gym Leader Challenge: special boss room with mentor figure — defeat them to unlock a permanent technique slot or stat bonus. One per zone, requires meeting style prerequisites (striker/grappler score thresholds).**
- **Zone Narrator: fight commentary system that reacts to combat events — "Beautiful combination!", "He's hurt!", "What a comeback!" — narrator lines scale with combo count and affect crowd mood (+5 hype per notable comment). Different voice packs per zone theme.**
- **Rival Crossroads: at midpoint of each zone, player chooses one of two paths — each leads to different encounter types and ultimately different boss archetypes. Creates meaningful replay value and strategic choice.**
- **Dynamic Spotlight: arena spotlights track the action — standing in spotlight grants +10% accuracy but enemies can use it to predict movement. Player can bait enemies into blind spots for advantage.**
- **Weight Cutting Mechanic: pre-fight weigh-in minigame — successfully cut water weight grants +10% power but -10% stamina for the zone. Risky reward for optimized builds.**
- **Clinic/Medical Bay: between zones, optional medical tent where player can spend earned currency to heal HP or remove persistent injury debuffs from the Injury System. Creates resource management choice between upgrading and healing.**

## Player (js/player.js)
- Skill tree: branching upgrades (striker vs grappler path)
- Equipment/gear system affecting stats
- Training mechanic: practice moves to level them up
- Stamina management depth (guard costs stamina)
- Health regeneration between zones
- **Style Gauge: tracks whether player prefers striking or grappling — reaching 100 in either unlocks a style-specific super move**
- **Signature Arsenal: after defeating a boss, permanently learn their signature technique (boss's best move added to player's move pool)**
- **Signature Stance System: unlock stance variants that change movement and counter timing — aggressive (faster dodge), defensive (wider block), balanced (chi regen boost)**
- **Training Montage Minigame: pre-zone preparation screen with button-timing challenges — success = +5% to random stat for next 3 rooms. Three difficulty tiers: Easy (single button), Medium (combo sequence), Hard (rapid mash).**
- **Second Wind Surge: at 15% HP, trigger a temporary 3-second burst — +40% attack speed, +20% movement, immune to knockback. Has 60s cooldown. Visual: player glows white/gold.**
- **Signature Discovery System: hidden emergent technique discovery — landing specific combo sequences (e.g., Jab-Jab-Uppercut) in a single fight reveals new techniques. Different sequences unlock different moves. Tiered reveals: basic combos first, advanced sequences after multiple fights.**
- **Combo Muscle Memory: repeated use of the same combo chain reduces its input delay by 5ms per use (capped at 50ms reduction). Rewards player mastery of chosen style.**
- **Fighting Style Synergy: using complementary moves (e.g., strike into grapple, or defensive counter into aggressive follow-up) builds synergy meter. At 100%, next special move costs 0 Focus and deals 1.5x damage.**
- **Signature Counter System: each fighting style has unique counter mechanics — striker counters have longer range but tighter timing (200ms window), grappler counters heal 5 HP on successful counter but have shorter range. Unlocks at Style Gauge 50+.**
- **Fight IQ Progression: after each boss defeat, player unlocks passive "reading" abilities — at 3 bosses, anticipate enemy attacks 10% faster; at 6 bosses, gain slight prediction on enemy attack patterns; at all bosses, enter "fight flow" where stamina costs reduce 15% permanently.**
- **Corner Bond System: between rooms in arena zones, your corner man's advice creates relationship points. After 5+ sessions, unlock "Corner Wisdom" passive — chosen advice path grants permanent stat bonus (+5% damage/aggressive, +8% defense/safe, or +15% stamina regen/endurance).**
- **Fight Record Book: tracks detailed combat history — longest combo, total damage, enemies defeated by type, win/loss record per zone. Unlocks "Hall of Fame" after milestone thresholds with unique rewards.**
- **Personal Rival System: track which enemy type has defeated player most — that type becomes a "rival" with special flavor text and unique drop table. Defeating rival enemies has chance to drop "Rival's" equipment with bonus stats.**
